# Guia de Estilo Front-end

## Layout

Os designs foram criados para as seguintes larguras:

- Mobile: 375px
- Desktop: 1440px

> 💡 Estas são apenas as dimensões do design. Garanta que o conteúdo seja responsivo e atenda aos requisitos  Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) testando em toda a gama de tamanhos de tela, de 320px até telas grandes.

## Cores

### Primária

- Navy 850 (intro and email sign up background): hsl(217, 28%, 15%)
- Navy 900 (main background): hsl(218, 28%, 13%)
- Navy 950 (footer background): hsl(216, 53%, 9%)
- Navy 800 (testimonials background): hsl(219, 30%, 18%)

### Destaque

- Teal 200 (inside call-to-action gradient): hsl(176, 68%, 64%)
- Cyan 500 (inside call-to-action gradient): hsl(198, 60%, 50%)
- Red 500 (error): hsl(0, 100%, 63%)

### Neutra

- White: hsl(0, 0%, 100%)
